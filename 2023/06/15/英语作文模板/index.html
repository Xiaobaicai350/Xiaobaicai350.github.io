词缀非常的多，而且有的词缀不只一个词性，我给大家的只是一部分，记住，在背单词的过程中也要不断的巩固掌握。
词性主要根据词缀判断
名词（n.):-ion、-ence、-ance、-sion、-ity、ment、-ism、-ation、-ancy、-ency、-ing
动词(v.):-ate、-ify、-ize、-en、-s、-ing
形容词（adj.):-al、-ent/ant、-able/ible、-ic、ous、-ing、-ed、-ive、-ory
副词(adv.)：ly

一个句子里面只能有一个动词，如果出现了n+v+n+__+n 那这个空应该填非谓语动词， 这个好像高中的时候学过

作文

议论文：问题解决

标志词：how to

三个段落：11句话

第一个段落

1-2 提出问题

3 过渡句

第二个段落 6句话 第一句：论点1.第二局：论据1……

第三段 2句话 第一句话重申观点 第二句话提出期望（万能结尾）

eg:The importance of speaking ability and how to improve it

第一段
1.With the rapid development of (education,society,technology,culture,economy,medical service医疗),
it is of great necessity for youngsters to improve our speaking ability.
2.过渡The following solutions can account for（解决） this phenomenon现象/issue问题
第二段
3.论点一：First and foremost,there is no doubt that we should pratice it more.
4.论据一：Based on a most recent survey,it is revealed(揭露) that a majority of successful social elites（精英） admit that they prefer practicing new things(oral English英语口语)
5.论点二：Furthermore,it is obvious that it is beneficial for students to make foreign friend
6.论据二：万能谚语（有志者事竟成）(where there is a will, there is a way.)
where there are friends ,there is communication.有朋友的地方就有交流
7.论点三：Last but not least,no one can deny(否认) that it is high time that we can travel to an English speaking country.
8.论据三： Only in this way,can we experience culture ourselves(have a better life).
第三段
9.重申观点：In a word ,it is clear that we should promote our speaking ability.
10.If we try our utmost(最大的) to develop our spoken English,the future of our life will be both promising and rosy.

议论文：观点选择

标志词：option

eg:Suppose you have two options upon graduation:one is to find a job somewhere and the other to start a business of your own

第一段：阐述问题，列出不同观点，做出选择，过渡句

  1. With the rapid development of (education,society,technology,culture,economy,medical service医疗),
    nowadays youngsters/students have different options on graduation .
  2. Some thinks that to find a job some where is beneficial,while others think to start a business of your own is a better choice.
  3. Personally,i prefer the former/latter.
  4. The following reasons can account for my preference.

第二段：

  1. First and foremost,there is no doubt that to find a job somewhere is more free.
  2. Based on a most recent survey,it is revealed that a majority of successful social elites（精英） admit that their careers are free
  3. Furthermore,it is obvious that it is beneficial for making friends
  4. where there are jobs/work places,there are friends .有地方就有朋友
  5. Last but not least,no one can deny that to find a job is easier than to start a company
  6. Though some people do succeed in starting a business of their own ,most of people failed when they just start

第三段：

  1. In a word ,it is clear that A is better/more suitable than B.
  2. If we try our utmost to work hard in our career,the future of our life will be both promising and rosy.

议论文：现象解释

标志词：what why

eg:The challenges of living in a big city(直接告诉你现象)

第一段：阐述背景，提出观点，过渡句子

  1. With the rapid development of (education,society,technology,culture,economy,medical service医疗),
  2. there is an increasing number of citizens moving from rural area to urban area.
  3. There are a large number of challenges of living in a big cities.
  4. The followings are some of them.

第二段：

  1. First and foremost,there is no doubt that the cost of housing will be a huge problem.
  2. Based on a most recent survey,it is revealed that a majority of successful social elites（精英） admit that they have a huge expense on housing
  3. Furthermore,it is obvious that the competition is stronger in big cities
  4. where there are people,there is competition .有人就有竞争
  5. Last but not least,no one can deny that the traffic condition in big city is worse than in rural area
  6. Although the road are wider in big cities,there are more vehicle
